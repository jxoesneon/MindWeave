# Ethical Monetization: Donation-First

MindWeave prioritizes user wellbeing over profit while maintaining sustainability.

## 💰 The Model
1.  **100% Free Core**: Binaural beat generation, all presets, and basic timers are always free.
2.  **Donation-First**: Support via **GitHub Sponsors** and **Open Collective**.
3.  **Dormant Ads**: Ads are off by default. They can be remotely enabled for a percentage of users if funding goals are not met.

## 🤝 Support Channels
- **In-App One-Time**: Small gifts ($3, $5, $10).
- **Monthly Supporters**: Sustaining development ($2/mo+).
- **GitHub Sponsors**: For developer-centric contributions (0% fees).
- **Open Collective**: Full financial transparency.

## 🛡️ User Protection
- No ads during active sessions.
- Clear indicators when ads are enabled.
- One-time "Remove Ads" purchase for long-term supporters.
- Privacy-first: No tracking of personal meditative data.
