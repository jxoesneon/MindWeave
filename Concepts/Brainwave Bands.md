# Scientific Foundation: Brainwave Bands

MindWeave is built on peer-reviewed research for brainwave entrainment.

## 📊 Frequency Bands

| Band | Range | Physiological Correlation |
|------|-------|---------------------------|
| **Delta** | 0.5 - 4 Hz | Deep sleep, physical healing, detached awareness |
| **Theta** | 4 - 8 Hz | Meditation, creativity, dream-like states, memory |
| **Alpha** | 8 - 12 Hz | Relaxed focus, stress reduction, calm alertness |
| **Beta** | 12 - 30 Hz | Logical thinking, concentration, high-alertness |
| **Gamma** | 30 - 100 Hz | Peak mental performance, high-level cognition |

## 🧪 Scientific Principles
- **Binaural Beats**: Presenting two different frequencies to each ear results in the brain perceiving a third beat frequency (the difference).
    - *Example*: 200Hz (Left) + 210Hz (Right) = 10Hz (Alpha) entrainment.
- **Carrier Frequency**: The base tones used to deliver the beats. Optimal range is 100-500Hz for maximum efficacy.
- **Entrainment**: The brain's tendency to synchronize its internal frequency with the external auditory stimulus.
